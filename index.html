Neural Networks Adversarial Robustness Evaluation

Author: AiEngAhmed

Abstract

This research evaluates the robustness of several neural network architectures against adversarial attacks by analyzing the performance under different training conditions (standard, smooth, and adversarial). We use FGSM, CW, and PGD attacks to test the models across three datasets.
